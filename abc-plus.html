ABC Plus

Like ABC Lite, ABC Plus contains the simplicity and visual stimulation used to help children with Cortical Visual Impairment (CVI) learn their ABCs — but also includes more educational content.

You'll find the same tap, listen, and learn experience as ABC Lite, with the addition of lowercase letters, numbers, and shapes — all on a black or white backdrop with bold red, yellow, or blue.

We can't wait for you to enjoy this app with your child, and we hope it helps your child learn to see so they can see to learn. #icanlearntosee
