Gridables Math

Gridables is a friendly worksheet companion that helps kids practice addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division — right on their device.

Scan a printed worksheet with your camera (or import a photo), then solve each problem using built-in visual tools: a number grid, number line, base-ten blocks, bead counter, and tally marks. It's built for kids who benefit from seeing the math, not just the numbers.

Like all Bright Little Eyes apps, Gridables doesn't collect or transmit any personal information — everything stays on your device.
