Drag n Drop

Drag n Drop is an app engineered to assist children who struggle to learn with cortical vision.

Using a simple design — either a black or white background with primary-colored objects — children with CVI can interact with the app without becoming overstimulated. On a black background, the child's eyes are drawn straight to the brightly colored objects.

This app encourages children with CVI to organize items of different primary colors into a container, such as a jar or piggy bank.

We can't wait for you to enjoy this app with your child, and we hope it helps your child learn to see so they can see to learn. #icanlearntosee
