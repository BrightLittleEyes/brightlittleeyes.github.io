Scratch Spelling

Scratch Spelling is a spelling companion in the Scratch family — helping kids practice words with the same simple, high-contrast design built for children with CVI and low vision.

Like all Bright Little Eyes apps, Scratch Spelling doesn't collect or transmit any personal information — everything stays on your device.
