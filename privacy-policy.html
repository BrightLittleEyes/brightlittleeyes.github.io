Privacy Policy for Scratch Math

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Scratch Math ("the App") is developed by Bright Little Eyes. This policy explains how the App handles information.

No Data Collection

Scratch Math does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to us or to any third party. The App does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking software of any kind.

Information Stored on Your Device

The App may store information such as a child's first name, worksheets, and scanned or photographed pages locally on your device, so the App can function and remember your progress. This information stays on your device, is never uploaded anywhere, and is deleted if you delete the App.

Camera and Photo Access

If you choose to scan or import a worksheet using your device's camera or photo library, those images are processed entirely on your device and are not sent to us or any third party.

App Store Update Check

The App may periodically check the App Store to see if a newer version is available. This check sends only the App's identifier (not any personal or device-identifying information) to Apple's servers.

Children's Privacy

Scratch Math is designed for use by children under adult supervision. Because the App does not collect any personal information, it complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at brightlittleeyes@gmail.com.
