Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a worksheet?
Tap the worksheet/scan icon, then choose "Take a Photo" to scan a printed worksheet with your camera, or "Choose from Library" to import a photo you've already saved.

Is my child's information private?
Yes. Scratch Math doesn't collect or transmit any personal information. Everything stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I use Scratch Math for addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division?
Yes — Scratch Math includes tools for all four operations, including a visual grid, number line, base-ten blocks, bead counter, and more.
